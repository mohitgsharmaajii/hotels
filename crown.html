THE CROWN PLAZA

Crowne Plaza® Okhla is a 5 star hotel located in the heart of corporate & industrial hub of Delhi, approximately 21 Kms from Indira Gandhi International Airport and city's domestic airport , offering integrated hospitality for business and leisure travelers. The hotel features 208 well-equipped guest rooms with complimentary Wi-Fi and a banqueting space of 8500 Sq. ft. Host events or meetings in one of the 11 indoor and 2 outdoor flexible avenues with the expertise of a dedicated Crowne Plaza® Meetings Director. Experience the magic of Italian and Chinese cuisine at ChaoBella, taste the world on a plate at Edesia, enjoy invigorating drinks at Copper Bar & drool worthy coffee and cakes at French Heart.Guests can rejuvenate their mind, body and soul at Spazzo, our wellness sanctuary encompassing of a Spa, Fitness Center,Salon & Outdoor Pool.
